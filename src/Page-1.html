About Jennifer

Favorite Things

My two favorite things to do in this world are to sing and dance. Watching "Dirty Dancing" for the first time at the age of three sparked my love for dance. Ever since I can remember, I've always loved choreographing dance numbers in my room and belting out ballads in the shower. I love breaking out into dance in the kitchen or living room and Rob is always right there with me, joining in with his goofy moves. Other hobbies of mine include community fitness, painting furniture, hiking, riding bikes, going to the beach, reading books, and baking. 

More about me

I know she'll care for our child in the same way.

Choosing adoption

Jenn's perspective

As a child who was adopted, I have a personal understanding of what it's like to be a part of a family through love, not blood. I was adopted as an infant in the Bronx, New York by my mother and father. Shortly after, my father passed away and my mother and I moved to Florida and into our own small apartment. We didn't have much but we had each other, and that was all I needed. My mother remarried and I gained a stepfather, who is now my "Dad", and then a sister, who gave me my wish of becoming a big sister.

When I was in second grade, my mother shared my adoption story with me. At first, I was confused, however I never felf upset or unloved. Being adopted made me feel special, like I was chosen. I feel that way to this day. Even though I was curious, my birth mother is still unknown to me and I have no resentment towards her. She chose what was best for me and gave me a chance at a loving family. I want this same experience for our child. I want him or her to feel special, chosen, and loved. I feel confident that I will be able to relate to and provide comfort for our child when it comes to questions and feelings regarding his or her adoption.
